Questions You Might Have

What will I actually receive when I sign up?

You’ll be added to Anni’s early access list and receive occasional emails with practical gut health insights, updates on new resources, and early notification of offers and course launches. No daily emails — only what’s genuinely useful.

How is this different from other gut health content I’ve already tried?

Gut Health Guidance is built on clinical evidence and real patient experience — not trends, detox culture, or supplement sales. Anni is an HCPC-registered dietitian with 10+ years of NHS and private practice experience. Everything here is designed to inform and empower, not overwhelm or restrict.

When will the platform resources actually be available?

The platform is currently in development. New resources — quizzes, guides, and courses — will be released in stages. Subscribers on the early access list will always be first to know and receive any founding member pricing before resources open to the public.

Is this a substitute for seeing a doctor or dietitian?

No. The content on Gut Health Guidance is educational and informational — it doesn’t replace individual medical advice. If your symptoms are worsening or concerning you, please speak with your GP or a specialist. See the full Disclaimer.
